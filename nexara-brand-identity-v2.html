01 — Logo System

The Nexara Mark — v2

The original 6-node mark was redesigned. Six explicit nodes implied exactly six domains — architecturally false for a 29-standard platform. The new mark is a convergence ring system: three concentric arcs of increasing precision, collapsing inward to a single radiant core. Infinite standards. One truth. The geometry is open — the arcs never complete — signalling ongoing expansion and the living nature of the platform.

Outer arc — open, dashed

The closest ring to truth. Solid, confident. This is where Nexara lives: the disciplined, precise core of compliance intelligence. Almost complete — always within reach of perfection.

Single source of truth. The AI intelligence layer. All data flows to this point. Pure white: clarity, certainty, the end of complexity. Radiates blue-to-teal outward — intelligence in motion.
Radial spokes — signal flow
Subtle directional lines from core to outer arc. Not nodes — no implied count. They suggest data pathways: the flow of information across every module, every standard, every API.

Sector verticals are deep, industry-specific systems that sit on top of the core — each with its own colour token and sub-brand treatment. The wordmark is shared; the accent colour and descriptor change.

06 — Brand Voice

How Nexara Speaks

Unchanged in principle. Upgraded for scale: Nexara now speaks with the authority of a platform that has no peer. That changes the register slightly — less "we solve your problem," more "this is the category."

Categorical
Nexara doesn't compete — it defines. "The only platform with 29 ISO standards, Finance, CRM, ESG, and HR in a single system." State the category. Own it.
Precise
Numbers over adjectives. "29 standards" not "comprehensive." "57 services" not "extensive." "£29/user" not "affordable." Precision is authority.
Confident
No hedging. No "can help with aspects of." Nexara runs your compliance. Nexara is your IMS. Nexara processes your payroll. Own the verb.
Sector-aware
When speaking to automotive buyers, speak IATF. When speaking to medtech, speak 13485. Nexara speaks every industry's language — demonstrate it.
Human
ISO audits are stressful. CSRD reporting is overwhelming. Payroll errors are embarrassing. Nexara understands the human cost of operational complexity.
AI-earned
Never "AI-powered." Show the AI: "Nexara flagged the IATF deviation before your Tier 1 customer did." Results, not promises. Capability, not features.

08 — Developer Brand (New)

@nexara/sdk

New in v2. The README exposes a public SDK package: @nexara/sdk. This is a brand touchpoint. Developers who integrate Nexara data into their own tools carry the Nexara name into their codebases. The SDK identity follows the core brand with developer-specific tone: precise, terse, trustworthy.

SDK Brand Treatment
nexara
sdk
The SDK sub-brand uses the same mark, standard wordmark, and a sdk descriptor in teal DM Mono. The teal accent signals the AI/developer layer throughout the brand system.
