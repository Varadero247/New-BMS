**[CLAUSE NUMBER].1 Assignment of Work Product.** The Contractor agrees that all work product, deliverables, inventions, discoveries, developments, improvements, source code, software, databases, designs, specifications, documentation, and any other materials created, conceived, or developed by the Contractor in connection with the Services (“Work Product”), whether alone or jointly with others, shall be the exclusive property of Nexara DMCC (“Nexara”).

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].2 Automatic Vesting.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, all intellectual property rights (including copyright, database rights, design rights, patent rights, and all other proprietary rights) in the Work Product shall automatically vest in and be owned by Nexara from the moment of creation.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].3 Assignment.** To the extent that any Work Product does not automatically vest in Nexara, the Contractor hereby irrevocably assigns to Nexara, with full title guarantee, all right, title, and interest in and to the Work Product, effective as of the date of creation.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].4 Moral Rights Waiver.** The Contractor hereby irrevocably and unconditionally waives, to the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, all moral rights (including rights of attribution and integrity) in the Work Product.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].5 No Competing Use.** The Contractor shall not use, copy, modify, distribute, or incorporate any Work Product into any product, service, or project other than Nexara’s without the prior written consent of Nexara. The Contractor agrees not to use information, methodologies, or architectures learned during the engagement to build or assist in building products that compete with the Nexara IMS Platform for a period of **two (2) years** following the end of the engagement.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].6 Pre-existing IP.** The Contractor shall identify in writing, prior to commencement of Services, any pre-existing intellectual property (“Background IP”) that the Contractor intends to incorporate into the Work Product. Nexara shall receive a perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free licence to use such Background IP to the extent incorporated in the Work Product. Unidentified Background IP incorporated without disclosure shall be deemed assigned to Nexara under this clause.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].7 Confidentiality.** The Contractor acknowledges that all Work Product, source code, technical specifications, database schemas, pricing information, business strategies, and other information relating to Nexara’s business constitute Confidential Information and Trade Secrets of Nexara. The Contractor shall:
  (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;
  (b) not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party without Nexara’s prior written consent;
  (c) use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing the Services;
  (d) upon termination of the engagement, promptly return or permanently destroy all materials containing Confidential Information and provide written certification thereof within **seven (7) days**.

  These obligations shall survive termination of the engagement for a period of **five (5) years**, or indefinitely for information constituting a trade secret.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].8 Further Assurance.** The Contractor shall, at Nexara’s reasonable request and expense, promptly execute any further documents or instruments and take any steps necessary to perfect, record, or enforce Nexara’s ownership of the Work Product in any jurisdiction.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].9 Remedies.** The Contractor acknowledges that breach of this clause would cause irreparable harm to Nexara for which monetary damages may be inadequate, and that Nexara shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief, specific performance, and any other equitable remedies available without proof of actual damage and without posting bond.

**[CLAUSE NUMBER].10 Governing Law.** This clause, and any dispute arising from it, shall be governed by the laws of the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC), United Arab Emirates.
