# Module Owner Training Programme — Overview

**Nexara IMS | Version 1.0 | Five One-Day Programmes | 7 CPD Hours Each**

---

## Purpose

The Module Owner Training Programme (MOTP) prepares compliance professionals, department heads, and operational managers to own and operate specific Nexara IMS modules at a proficient level. Participants leave each programme able to create, manage, close, and report on all records within their designated module group — and to generate ISO-ready evidence packages without administrator assistance.

## Programme Structure

The MOTP comprises five independent one-day programmes. Participants attend only the programme(s) relevant to their role. Days A–E can be delivered in any order and on consecutive or non-consecutive days.

| Programme | Slug | Audience | Modules Covered |
|-----------|------|----------|----------------|
| Day A — Quality & NC | `quality-nc` | Quality managers, document controllers, QMS coordinators | Quality workflows, Document control, Non-conformance management, CAPA linkage |
| Day B — HSE | `hse` | HSE managers, EHS coordinators, safety officers | Incident recording, Legal register, Environmental aspects, PTW, Inspections |
| Day C — HR & Payroll | `hr-payroll` | HR managers, payroll administrators, L&D coordinators | Employee records, Payroll periods, Training tracker, Compliance matrix |
| Day D — Finance & Contracts | `finance-contracts` | Finance managers, procurement leads, legal counsel | Financial records, Contract lifecycle, Supplier management, PO workflow |
| Day E — Advanced | `advanced` | Audit leads, QMS managers, management review secretaries | Audit programme, CAPA root cause, Management review, ISO evidence |

## Daily Schedule (All Five Days — 08:30–17:00)

| Time | Session | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| 08:30–09:00 | Welcome, group introductions, day objectives | 30 min |

| 10:45–12:15 | Content Block 2 — Second module deep-dive | 90 min |
| 12:15–13:00 | Lunch | 45 min |

**Total contact time**: 7 hours (excluding breaks and lunch) = **7 CPD hours per programme**

## Assessment Design

| Criterion | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Format | 20 MCQ per day |
| Time | 30 minutes (timed; timer runs in the web portal) |
| Pass threshold | ≥ 75% (15/20 correct) |
| Fail | Retake at next scheduled cohort |
| Grade | Pass or Fail — no distinction grade for one-day programmes |
| Certificate | Issued same day on pass; printed and presented at certificate ceremony |
| No Part B | Module owners operate the system; administrators configure it. Scenario-based Part B not required. |

## CPD Accreditation

Each programme carries **7 CPD hours** accredited under the Nexara IMS continuing professional development framework. CPD certificates are included with the completion certificate and may be submitted to professional bodies including:

- IOSH (Institution of Occupational Safety and Health)
- CQI/IRCA (Chartered Quality Institute)
- CIPD (Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development)
- CIMA / ICAEW (finance and accounting bodies)
- IIA (Institute of Internal Auditors)

## Prerequisites

Participants should have:

- Active Nexara IMS user account with appropriate module permissions
- Basic computer literacy and familiarity with browser-based applications
- (Recommended but not required) Prior attendance at the End User Training Programme

Administrator training is **not** a prerequisite for module owner training. These programmes are designed to be taken independently.

## Programme Materials Included

Each day programme includes:

- Facilitator schedule with energy notes and timing cues
- Full facilitation script with transition language
- 2–3 module content documents (200–400 words each section, worked examples, field names, common mistakes)
- Hands-on lab exercise with scenario backstory, 7 numbered steps, and debrief questions
- 20-question assessment with separate facilitator-only answer key
- Participant handbook section and quick-reference card variant
- Virtual and on-site delivery guidance
