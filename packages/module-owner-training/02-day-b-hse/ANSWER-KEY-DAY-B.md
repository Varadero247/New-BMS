| 1 | B | A near miss — the event occurred and could have caused injury but did not. An observation would be a pre-existing condition (e.g. trailing cable not yet involved in an event). |
| 2 | C | `dateOccurred` = when the incident happened. The report date is a separate field (`dateReported`). Accurate timing is critical for incident trend analysis and regulatory timelines. |

| 5 | B | TRIR = (incidents × 200,000) ÷ hours worked. 200,000 = 100 workers × 50 weeks × 40 hours. This is the OSHA and ISO standard denominator. |
