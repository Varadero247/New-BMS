## Part 1: Programme Overview

### Your Learning Journey

The Module Owner Training Programme is built around one core idea: **you already know your domain.** You know what quality means, what safety means, what good HR looks like. This programme teaches you how Nexara IMS captures, evidences, and reports on the work you already do — and how to make that evidence count when an auditor walks in.

By the end of your programme day, you will:
- Navigate your module confidently without guidance
- Complete the full record lifecycle from creation to closure
- Generate the ISO evidence package your next audit needs
- Know exactly which reports to run and when

### Assessment and Certificate

Each programme day ends with a 20-question multiple-choice assessment. You have 30 minutes. 75% is the pass mark. The questions test the content covered during the day — if you've worked through the lab, you'll find the questions recognisable.

Certificates are presented at the end of each day. CPD hours (7 per day) are confirmed on a separate CPD slip.
