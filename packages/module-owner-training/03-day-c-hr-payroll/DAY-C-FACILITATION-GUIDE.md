## 09:00 — Content Block 1: Employee Lifecycle

**Key facilitation point — GDPR**: HR professionals will ask about GDPR frequently. Key points:
- The system stores personal data lawfully under the "performance of a contract" basis for employees
- Retention periods are configurable per jurisdiction — for UK GDPR, employment records + 7 years is standard
- Right-to-erasure requests go through a review workflow because statutory retention requirements may override the request

**Common question**: "Can line managers see employee salary information?"
Answer: No — salary and payroll data is restricted to HR Admin and Payroll roles. Line managers with standard HR access see attendance, performance, and training records only.

## 13:00 — Content Block 3: Payroll

**Opening**: "Payroll is where HR and finance meet. Today we're focusing on the management of payroll records and the audit trail — not the payroll calculations themselves. What matters for module owners is: can you prove that the right people were paid the right amounts for the right reasons, with appropriate sign-off at each stage?"

**Variance report emphasis**: "The variance report is your pre-flight check before the journal export. If payroll goes up 20% month-on-month and you haven't approved a large bonus, something is wrong. Always run variance before export."

## Q&A Management

| Question | Answer |
|---------|--------|
| "Can we integrate Nexara Payroll with our existing payroll system?" | "The journal export is specifically designed for integration — it exports in formats compatible with most major finance systems. Your Nexara administrator configures the chart of accounts mapping." |
| "What happens if we need to process a payroll correction after the period is Paid?" | "Create a manual correction in the following period with a reference to the original period. The system maintains a complete audit trail. Never modify a Paid period record." |
| "Can employees see their own payslips in Nexara?" | "Yes — if the employee portal module is activated. Employees can view and download their payslips via the self-service portal. Your Nexara administrator activates this feature." |
