# Day C — HR & Payroll: Full Day Schedule

**Programme**: Nexara Certified Module Owner — HR & Payroll
**Duration**: 08:30–17:00 | 7 CPD Hours
**Audience**: HR managers, payroll administrators, L&D coordinators, HR business partners
**Maximum Group Size**: 14 participants

---

## Facilitator Energy Note

HR and payroll professionals are highly privacy-conscious. They may initially be cautious about entering employee data into a sandbox environment — reassure them immediately: the sandbox uses entirely fictional employee data and is wiped weekly. This group tends to be process-oriented and will value clarity on exactly which fields are mandatory vs recommended. Energy is consistent throughout the day; the payroll section may feel procedural — ground it in audit trail storytelling.

---

## Full Timetable

### 08:30–09:00 | Welcome & Orientation
- Introductions: name, organisation, role, one current HR/payroll challenge
- Sandbox login: Meridian Healthcare Services sample company (fictional employees pre-loaded)
- Reassurance on data privacy: "All data in the sandbox is fictional. No real employee information is used."

### 09:00–10:30 | Content Block 1 — Employee Record Lifecycle (90 min)
**Session aim**: Participants manage the full employee lifecycle from starter to leaver

Topics (see `MODULE-HR-WORKFLOWS.md`):
- Employee record structure and mandatory fields (20 min)
- Starter process: onboarding checklist, document uploads, role assignment (25 min)
- Absence management: absence types, recording, return-to-work process (20 min)
- Leaver process: exit checklist, termination, data retention obligations (15 min)
- Contractor management: end-dates, on-site access period, IR35 flag (10 min)

**Transition**: "After the break, we'll look at the training and compliance side of HR — where you track whether your people have completed mandatory training."

### 10:30–10:45 | Break

### 10:45–12:15 | Content Block 2 — Training Tracker & Compliance (90 min)
Topics (see `MODULE-TRAINING-TRACKER.md`):
- Course management: creating and configuring training courses in Nexara (20 min)
- Individual and group assignment: single-user vs department bulk assignment (20 min)
- Completion recording: dates, results, certificate uploads (15 min)
- Compliance matrix report: overdue assignments, completion percentages by department (20 min)
- Deadline alerts: configuring email reminders for upcoming training deadlines (15 min)

**Transition**: "After lunch, we'll look at the payroll side — not the payroll calculations themselves, but the records, audit trail, and finance export that HR and payroll managers need to manage."

### 12:15–13:00 | Lunch

### 13:00–14:15 | Content Block 3 — Payroll Management (75 min)
Topics (see `MODULE-PAYROLL-MANAGEMENT.md`):
- Pay period structure and initiation (20 min)
- Pay adjustments: overtime, bonuses, deductions, benefit-in-kind (25 min)
- Payroll audit trail: who approved what, when, and why (15 min)
- Journal export for finance system integration (15 min)

### 14:15–14:30 | Break

### 14:30–15:45 | Lab — Hands-On Scenario (75 min)
**Lab file**: `LAB-DAY-C.md` — Meridian Healthcare: Onboard nurse → assign training → run payroll period → generate compliance report

### 15:45–16:30 | KPI Dashboards & Reporting (45 min)
- HR analytics: headcount trend, absence rate, training completion %
- Payroll variance report
- GDPR: data retention check and right-to-erasure demonstration

### 16:30–17:00 | Assessment + Certificate Ceremony
Assessment: 20 MCQ, 30 min, ≥ 75% → `/module-owner/hr-payroll/assessment`
Certificate: "Nexara Certified Module Owner — HR & Payroll" | CPD: 7 hours (CIPD accepted)
