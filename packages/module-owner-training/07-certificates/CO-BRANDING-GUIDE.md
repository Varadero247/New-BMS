# Co-Branding Guide — Module Owner Training Certificates

---

## What Is Permitted

Client organisations may add their organisation's logo and name to:
1. Programme delivery slides (bottom-right corner of each slide, below the Nexara logo)
2. Participant certificates (bottom-right co-branding zone — see CERTIFICATE-SPEC.md)
3. Participant handbooks (cover page, inside back cover)
4. Email communications about the programme (footer area)

---

## Logo Placement Rules

### On Certificates
- **Position**: Bottom-right co-branding zone (as marked in the certificate layout spec)
- **Maximum size**: 40mm wide × 20mm tall (must fit within the co-branding zone)
- **Minimum size**: No smaller than 25mm wide
- **Spacing**: Minimum 5mm separation from the Nexara seal element
- **Format**: High-resolution PNG or SVG (no JPEGs)

### On Slides
- **Position**: Bottom-right corner of every slide — below and to the right of the Nexara logo
- **Size**: Proportional to Nexara logo; never larger than the Nexara logo
- **Never on the same line** as the Nexara logo

---

## What Is NOT Permitted

The following modifications are explicitly prohibited:

1. **Removing the Nexara logo** from any material — the Nexara logo must always be present and prominent
2. **Resizing or distorting the Nexara logo** — always use original aspect ratio
3. **Changing the Nexara colour palette** — do not apply client brand colours to Nexara-branded elements
4. **Adding a second co-brand logo** from a third party (e.g. a consultancy the client has engaged)
5. **Modifying any programme content** — question banks, pass thresholds, CPD hour declarations, certificate titles
6. **Replacing the certificate title** — the certificate must always read "Nexara Certified Module Owner — [Programme]"
7. **Adding the client's name as the issuing authority** — the issuing authority is always "Nexara DMCC / Nexara Training Academy"
8. **Adding a client-issued CPD accreditation** in addition to or instead of the Nexara CPD accreditation

---

## Approved Co-Branding Text on Certificates

The following optional text may be added to the bottom of the certificate, below the co-branding zone:

> "Delivered in partnership with [Client Organisation Name]"

This text must be in 8pt Montserrat Regular, slate grey (#6B7280). No other text may be added.

---

## Organisation Name on Certificate

The participant's organisation name may be included on the certificate in the "Cohort" field:

> Cohort: [Client Organisation Name] — [Month Year]

---

## Approval Process

Client organisations wishing to use the co-branded template must:
1. Submit their logo files (PNG/SVG, min 300 DPI) to training@nexara.io at least 5 business days before the programme delivery date
2. Receive written approval of the co-branded template from Nexara Training
3. Not modify the approved template in any way after sign-off

Nexara reserves the right to withdraw co-branding approval if materials are used in violation of these guidelines.
