## Physical Format

- **Size**: A4 landscape (297mm × 210mm)
- **Paper**: 170gsm silk/gloss or heavier; laser-print quality minimum
- **Orientation**: Landscape only — never portrait
- **Colour mode**: Full colour (CMYK print; RGB digital)
- **Margin**: 20mm all sides

## Nexara Brand Elements

### Colours
- **Nexara Navy**: `#1E3A5F` — background elements, borders
- **Nexara Gold**: `#B8860B` — programme title, decorative elements, seal
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — primary text background
- **Dark text**: `#1A1A2E` — participant name and body text

### Typography
- **Certificate header** ("CERTIFICATE OF ACHIEVEMENT"): Montserrat Bold, 14pt, letter-spaced 3px, Navy
- **Certifying text** ("This certifies that..."): Montserrat Regular, 12pt, Dark
- **Participant name**: Montserrat Bold, 24pt, Navy
- **Programme title**: Montserrat SemiBold, 18pt, Gold
- **Detail fields**: Montserrat Regular, 10pt, Dark

### Logo
- Nexara logo (wordmark + symbol): top-left position; minimum 40mm wide; do not scale below 30mm
- Minimum clear space: 5mm all sides from any other element
- Never alter logo colours, proportions, or add effects

### Nexara Seal
- Circular embossed/foil seal (physical) or high-resolution PNG (digital)
- Position: bottom-right of the main content area, above the co-branding zone
- Diameter: 35mm

---

## Digital Certificate Format

For digital delivery (PDF):
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum
- Export as PDF/A-1b (ISO 19005-1 archival format)
- Filename convention: `nexara-cert-module-owner-{programme-slug}-{YYYYMMDD}-{participant-last-name}.pdf`
- QR code on digital certificates: links to certificate verification page

---

## Certificate Verification

All issued certificates are registered in the Nexara Training Registry. Verification URL: `https://verify.nexara.io/cert/{certificate-id}`

Certificate IDs are auto-generated by the web portal assessment system on pass. Format: `NMO-{PROGRAMME}-{YEAR}-{6-DIGIT-RANDOM}`. Example: `NMO-HSE-2026-481923`
