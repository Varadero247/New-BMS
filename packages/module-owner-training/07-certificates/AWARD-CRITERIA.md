# Award Criteria — Module Owner Training Certificates

---

## Pass Criteria

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Assessment score | ≥ 75% (15/20 correct on the 20-question MCQ) |
| Assessment completion | Within the 30-minute time limit |
| Attendance | Full-day attendance at the relevant programme |
| Lab participation | Active participation in the hands-on lab exercise |

All four criteria must be met for a certificate to be issued.

---

## Grading

Module Owner Training programmes are graded **Pass or Fail only** — there is no Distinction grade.

This reflects the one-day nature of each programme. The Administrator Training Programme (two-day programme) awards a Distinction for ≥ 90%; Module Owner programmes do not include this grade as the assessment is shorter and the learning is more operationally focused.

| Score | % | Grade |
|-------|---|-------|
| 15/20 | 75% | Pass (minimum) |
| 16/20 | 80% | Pass |
| 17/20 | 85% | Pass |
| 18/20 | 90% | Pass |
| 19/20 | 95% | Pass |
| 20/20 | 100% | Pass |
| 14/20 or below | ≤70% | Fail |

---

## Same-Day Certificate Issuance

Certificates are presented to passing participants at the end of the programme day, during the certificate ceremony (16:45–17:00 approximately). Facilitators should have certificates pre-printed before the programme begins.

**Do not print certificates with participant names before confirming attendance** — late cancellations are common. Have blank certificates ready for completion by hand, or print digitally using the portal's certificate generation function.

### Digital Certificates (Portal)

The web portal assessment system generates a digital certificate ID and a downloadable PDF on assessment pass. Participants can access their certificate immediately from the results screen. Physical certificates are presented additionally if the client has requested them.

---

## Fail Procedure

For participants who do not pass:

1. Announce pass results only — never announce fails publicly
2. After the group has dispersed, speak privately with the participant
3. Issue a **Completion Acknowledgement** (attendance only — no assessment pass):
   - States that the participant attended the full-day programme
   - Does not claim any competence certification
   - Includes 7 CPD hours for attendance (CPD hours are not conditional on passing)
4. Discuss the retake process:
   - Retake available at the next scheduled cohort of the same programme day
   - No partial retake — the full programme day must be attended again
   - No limit on number of retakes
   - Recommend reviewing the relevant module content files and practicing in the sandbox before retaking

---

## CPD Hours

CPD hours are awarded for attendance, not for passing the assessment:

| Programme | CPD Hours | Accepted By |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Day A — Quality & NC | 7 hours | CQI/IRCA, ISO auditor bodies |
| Day B — HSE | 7 hours | IOSH, NEBOSH (self-reported), CQI |
| Day C — HR & Payroll | 7 hours | CIPD (self-reported) |
| Day D — Finance & Contracts | 7 hours | CIMA, ICAEW, IACCM (self-reported) |
| Day E — Audits, CAPA & MR | 7 hours | IIA, CQI/IRCA |

CPD slips are issued to all participants (including those who do not pass) on the day. CPD slips include: programme title, date, facilitator name, hours, and Nexara DMCC contact details for verification.

---

## Certificate Validity

Module Owner certificates do not expire. However, Nexara recommends refresher training when:
- A major new release of Nexara IMS introduces significant changes to the relevant modules (Nexara will communicate this to certificate holders via email)
- The participant's role significantly changes, requiring familiarity with different module aspects
- More than 3 years have elapsed since initial certification (recommended, not mandatory)
