# Day A — Quality & Non-Conformance: Full Day Schedule

**Programme**: Nexara Certified Module Owner — Quality & Non-Conformance
**Duration**: 08:30–17:00 | 7 CPD Hours
**Audience**: Quality managers, document controllers, QMS coordinators, internal auditors (quality focus)
**Maximum Group Size**: 14 participants

---

## Facilitator Energy Note

Quality professionals arrive energised and slightly competitive — they want to know where the edges of the system are. Use this energy in the morning to let them probe and test the system. By afternoon, channel it into the lab: their instinct to "get it right" produces excellent lab work. The assessment tends to be the group's strongest performance.

---

## Full Timetable

### 08:30–09:00 | Welcome & Orientation (30 min)

**Session aim**: Establish group rapport, set day objectives, calibrate expectations

**Activities**:
- Facilitator introduces themselves and Nexara training team (3 min)
- Round-table introductions: name, organisation, role, and "one quality challenge I deal with regularly" (15 min)
- Day A objectives and schedule walkthrough — point out lab and assessment timing (5 min)
- Sandbox environment login check — every participant must confirm access before 09:00 ends (5 min)
- Parking lot introduced — flip chart or shared doc for questions to address in lab/debrief (2 min)

**Facilitator note**: If more than one participant has a login issue, address as a group and delay Content Block 1 start by no more than 10 minutes. Contact IT support if sandbox is unavailable.

---

### 09:00–10:30 | Content Block 1 — Quality Workflows & Document Control (90 min)

**Session aim**: Participants understand the quality record lifecycle and can operate document control within Nexara

**Topics covered** (see `MODULE-QUALITY-WORKFLOWS.md` for full content):

- **Record management architecture** (20 min): How quality records are structured in Nexara — record types, reference number format (QMS-REC-YYYY-NNN), mandatory vs optional fields, and the audit trail that underpins every record change
- **Document control workflow** (25 min): Document creation, versioning (v1.0, v1.1, v2.0 scheme), review/approval routing, superseding old versions, and what ISO 9001 clause 7.5 requires that Nexara automates
- **Approval workflows** (20 min): Single and multi-stage approval routing, escalation rules, delegated authority, and how to re-route an approval if the primary approver is absent
- **Quality KPIs overview** (15 min): Introduction to the KPI dashboard — NC open rate, closure rate within SLA, CAPA effectiveness rate, repeat NC rate; what "red" means operationally
- **Q&A and parking lot** (10 min): Address questions; capture anything that requires a sandbox demonstration

**Transition cue**: "We've covered how quality records are structured and how documents flow through approval. After the break, we'll go deeper on the most time-consuming part of most quality managers' week — non-conformance management."

---

### 10:30–10:45 | Break (15 min)

---

### 10:45–12:15 | Content Block 2 — Non-Conformance Management (90 min)

**Session aim**: Participants can create, categorise, investigate, link CAPA, and close non-conformance records

**Topics covered** (see `MODULE-NC-MANAGEMENT.md` for full content):

- **NC creation and categorisation** (20 min): NC field walkthrough — title, description, category (Product/Process/System/Supplier), severity (Minor/Major/Critical), source (Customer complaint, Internal audit, External audit, Self-identified), detection point, responsible owner
- **Investigation workflow** (25 min): Immediate containment actions, investigation team assignment, contributing factor recording, root cause entry, and the link between NC investigation and CAPA creation; common mistake — confusing containment action with corrective action
- **CAPA linkage** (20 min): Creating a CAPA directly from the NC record (not standalone); understanding the NC-to-CAPA parent-child relationship; CAPA fields that must reference the NC
- **Closure verification** (15 min): Verification evidence requirements, who can approve closure, the re-opening process if closure evidence is insufficient, and the ISO 9001 evidence requirements for NC closure
- **Repeat NC tracking** (10 min): How the system identifies repeat NCs (same category, same root cause, same process area within 12 months); escalation triggers; reporting repeat NCs to senior management

**Transition cue**: "After lunch, we'll look at two things that take your NC and CAPA data and turn it into management insight — the KPI dashboard in depth and ISO evidence package generation."

---

### 12:15–13:00 | Lunch (45 min)

---

### 13:00–14:15 | Content Block 3 — Reporting, ISO Evidence & Dashboard Configuration (75 min)

**Session aim**: Participants can configure reports, interpret KPIs, and generate ISO evidence packages

**Topics covered**:

- **Quality KPI deep-dive** (25 min): NC open rate by category and severity; closure within SLA (30-day default, configurable); CAPA effectiveness rate; repeat NC rate; customising the dashboard view; exporting dashboard data
- **Report configuration** (25 min): Building a custom quality report — select data source (NC, CAPA, Documents), filters (date range, category, responsible owner, status), output format (PDF, CSV, Word); scheduling automated report delivery by email
- **ISO 9001 clause mapping** (15 min): How Nexara maps NC categories to ISO 9001:2015 clauses (8.7 — Control of nonconforming outputs; 10.2 — Nonconformity and corrective action); generating a clause-mapped evidence list for auditors
- **ISO evidence package generation** (10 min): Selecting scope (date range, module, clause), reviewing the evidence index, exporting the package as a ZIP file with PDF index

### 14:15–14:30 | Break (15 min)

---

### 14:30–15:45 | Lab — Hands-On Scenario Walkthrough (75 min)

**Session aim**: Participants complete a full NC-to-CAPA-to-closure workflow independently in the sandbox

**Lab file**: See `LAB-DAY-A.md` for full scenario, steps, expected outcomes, and debrief questions

**Facilitator actions**:
- Introduce the scenario (Greenfield Manufacturing — supplier NC on steel components) — 5 min
- Steps 1–2: Walk through together on facilitator screen — 10 min
- Steps 3–7: Participants work independently — facilitator circulates — 45 min
- Extension task: Early finishers configure the quality dashboard and schedule a report — 10 min
- Group debrief: 3 debrief questions — 5 min

---

### 15:45–16:30 | KPI Dashboards, Reports & Export (45 min)

**Session aim**: Participants leave knowing exactly which reports their auditor will want and how to produce them

**Activities**:
- Demonstration: generate a 90-day NC summary report and a CAPA effectiveness report (15 min)
- Demonstration: export ISO evidence package for a fictitious ISO 9001 surveillance audit (15 min)
- Participants replicate both exports in their sandbox (10 min)
- Q&A — address parking lot items from earlier in the day (5 min)

---

### 16:30–17:00 | Assessment + Certificate Ceremony (30 min)

**Assessment**: 20 MCQ, 30 minutes, ≥ 75% pass (see `ASSESSMENT-DAY-A.md`)

- Participants open the web portal assessment at `/module-owner/quality-nc/assessment`
- Timer starts immediately on entry
- Facilitator is available for technical issues only
- Results displayed immediately on submission

**Certificate ceremony** (after all submissions):
- Announce pass results (do not announce fail results publicly)
- Present "Nexara Certified Module Owner — Quality & Non-Conformance" certificates
- Issue CPD confirmation slips (7 hours) to all participants
- Brief participants who did not pass privately; confirm retake process

**Close**: Thank participants, confirm evaluation form submission, remind about post-training sandbox access (14 days).
