# Co-Branding Guide — Certificate Co-Branding

---

## Permitted Co-Branding

Clients may add their organisation logo to the Nexara Certified Platform Administrator certificate in one designated zone:

**Permitted zone**: Bottom-right corner only.

| Format | Maximum size |
|--------|-------------|
| Print (PDF, A4 landscape) | 80mm wide × 40mm tall |
| Digital (PNG, 1920×1080) | 160px wide × 80px tall |

### Permitted formats for client logo
- SVG (preferred — scales cleanly)
- PNG with transparent background
- JPEG (acceptable; white/transparent background required)

---

## Not Permitted

The following are explicitly prohibited:

| Prohibited action | Reason |
|------------------|--------|
| Adding client logo anywhere other than bottom-right zone | Breaks Nexara brand hierarchy |
| Replacing the Nexara logo with a client logo | The issuer is Nexara, not the client |
| Modifying the "NEXARA CERTIFIED" credential text | The credential is Nexara's intellectual property |
| Adding a second "Awarded by [Client Name]" statement | Could be interpreted as client issuing the certificate |
| Changing background colour or border | Breaks brand recognition |
| Adding promotional text (phone numbers, URLs, taglines) | Not permitted on credentials |
| Printing on client letterhead without using the template | Certificate must use official template |

---

## How to Add Your Logo (Web Portal)

1. Log into the training portal: `https://your-instance.nexara.io/training-portal`
2. Navigate to **Admin → Certificate Settings**
3. Upload your organisation logo (SVG or PNG recommended)
4. Preview the certificate to confirm placement
5. Save — all subsequent certificate exports will include your logo

The portal automatically:
- Centres the logo within the co-branding zone
- Adds white padding if the logo has no transparent background
- Scales the logo to fit within the permitted dimensions

---

## Adding Client Name

The certificate already includes:
> "representing **[ORGANISATION NAME]**"

This is the correct and only location where the client organisation name appears. It is NOT Nexara's branding — it identifies the participant's employer/organisation.

---

## Approval Process

Client logo files uploaded to the training portal are **auto-approved** (no manual review required) provided the file:
- Is uploaded by an Org Admin
- Does not exceed 2MB
- Is in a permitted format (SVG, PNG, JPEG)

Nexara reserves the right to remove client logos that violate these guidelines.
