# Award Criteria — Nexara Certified Platform Administrator

---

## Certificate Grades

| Grade | Assessment Score | Certificate Title |
|-------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Fail** | < 75% (< 42/55) | No certificate; retake required |
| **Pass** | 75–89% (42–49/55) | Nexara Certified Platform Administrator |
| **Distinction** | ≥ 90% (≥ 50/55) | Nexara Certified Platform Administrator with Distinction |

---

## Component Scores

| Component | Marks | Weighting |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Part A: 40 MCQ | 40 | 72.7% |
| Part B: 3 scenario questions × 5 | 15 | 27.3% |
| **Total** | **55** | **100%** |

There is no separate pass mark for Part A or Part B — only the combined total determines the grade.

---

## Attendance Requirement

To sit the summative assessment, participants must have:
- Attended both full days of the programme (or equivalent self-paced completion)
- Completed all 7 lab exercises (facilitator sign-off in COMPETENCY-CHECKLIST.md)
- Achieved Level 2 or above in at least 5 of 7 competency domains

Participants who did not complete labs must complete them before retaking the assessment.

---

## Retake Policy

| Attempt | Policy |
|---------|--------|
| 1st attempt | Standard assessment at end of Day 2 |
| 2nd attempt | Available at next scheduled cohort (no waiting period) |
| 3rd attempt | Available at next scheduled cohort after additional targeted coaching |
| 4th+ attempt | Requires Nexara Training Team approval; may require full programme retake |

Retake assessments use a different question set of equivalent difficulty.

---

## Certificate Validity

Nexara Certified Platform Administrator certificates do **not** expire automatically. However:

- Nexara recommends recertification every **3 years**
- A new major platform version (e.g., v3.0.0) may require recertification via an update module
- Nexara will notify certificate holders by email when recertification is recommended
- Recertification uses an abbreviated assessment (20 MCQ + 1 scenario, 30 min)

---

## Certificate Registry

All certificates are registered in the Nexara Training Registry with:
- Participant full name
- Organisation
- Certificate ID (UUID)
- Grade
- Assessment date
- Facilitator name
- Programme version

**Verification URL**: `https://certs.nexara.io/verify/{certificate-id}`

External parties (employers, auditors) can verify a certificate's authenticity and current status at this URL.

**Registry retention**: 7 years from certificate date.

---

## CPD Recognition

The programme awards **14 CPD hours** (2 full days). A separate CPD certificate is issued alongside the administrator certificate.

The CPD certificate is issued regardless of assessment outcome — it recognises participation in the learning programme, not the assessment result.

CPD certificates are available for download from the training portal immediately after programme completion.
