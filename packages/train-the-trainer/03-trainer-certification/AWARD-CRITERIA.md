# Award Criteria — Nexara Certified Internal Trainer

**Programme**: Nexara IMS Train-the-Trainer (T3)
**Version**: 1.0
**Credential**: Nexara Certified Internal Trainer — End User & Module Owner Programmes

---

## 1. Summary of Award Criteria

| Component | Threshold | Consequence if Not Met |
|-----------|-----------|----------------------|
| Written Assessment | ≥ 75% (15/20 MCQ) | Fail — see resubmission policy |
| Delivery Assessment | ≥ 70% overall average (≥ 2.5/4) AND domain minimums | Fail — see resubmission policy |
| Both Required | — | Credential not awarded until both pass |
| Annual Renewal | ≥ 70% on 10-MCQ refresher OR T3 Day 2 attendance | Credential lapses if renewal deadline missed |

---

## 2. Assessment Components

### 2.1 Written Assessment

**Format**: 20 MCQ, closed book, 30 minutes, individually administered
**Pass threshold**: ≥ 75% (15 out of 20 correct)
**Question coverage**: Adult learning (Q1–4), facilitation (Q5–8), Nexara curriculum (Q9–12), assessment delivery (Q13–16), programme management (Q17–20)
**When**: Day 2, 15:15–15:45
**Administered by**: Nexara Master Trainer (invigilated)
**Marked by**: Nexara Master Trainer using `ANSWER-KEY-WRITTEN.md`
**Result communicated**: Day 2, 15:45 individual debrief

### 2.2 Delivery Assessment

**Format**: 20-minute observed training delivery segment
**Pass threshold**: Overall average ≥ 2.5 out of 4 (70%) AND the following domain minimums:

| Domain | Minimum Score |
|--------|--------------|
| 1. Content Mastery | 2 |
| 2. Facilitation | 2 |
| 3. Assessment Administration | 2 |
| 4. Programme Management | 1 |
| 5. Professionalism | 2 |

**Scoring instrument**: `03-trainer-certification/DELIVERY-OBSERVER-CHECKLIST.md`
**Observer**: Nexara Master Trainer delivering the T3 programme
**When**: Day 2, 13:00–15:00 (20-minute slots per participant)
**Result communicated**: Day 2, 15:45 individual debrief (same session as written result)

## 3. Certification Outcomes

### Outcome 1: CERTIFIED

**Criteria**: Written ≥ 75% AND Delivery ≥ 70% overall AND all domain minimums met

**Actions taken**:
- Credential awarded: "Nexara Certified Internal Trainer — End User & Module Owner Programmes"
- Certificate generated via Training Portal (PDF; also available for printing)
- Trainer register updated by Nexara L&D by end of Day 2
- Curriculum handover package provided at end of Day 2
- Renewal date set: 12 months from the certificate issue date
- Renewal reminder: sent at 60 days and 14 days before expiry

---

### Outcome 2: CONDITIONAL (Written Pass; Delivery Fail)

**Criteria**: Written ≥ 75% AND (Delivery < 70% OR any domain minimum not met)

**What is banked**: Written result is valid for 30 days from the original T3 cohort date.

**Actions taken**:
- Credential NOT awarded on Day 2
- Participant receives an individual debrief explaining the delivery score by domain and the specific development areas
- Nexara Master Trainer submits a resubmission request to training@nexara.io by end of Day 2
- Resubmission session scheduled within 30 days (virtual or on-site)
- Participant delivers a **different** 20-minute segment (not the same segment as the original assessment)
- Same observer checklist and pass criteria apply

**Resubmission outcomes**:
- Pass: Credential awarded; backdated to original T3 cohort date
- Fail: Full retake required at next scheduled T3 cohort

**Extension**: A one-time 14-day extension is available if the participant cannot attend within 30 days due to documented circumstances (illness, emergency travel). The extension request must be made before the 30-day deadline expires and approved by the Nexara Head of L&D.

---

### Outcome 3: FULL RETAKE (Written Fail; Delivery Any Result)

**Criteria**: Written < 75% (regardless of delivery result)

**Important**: Delivery pass does NOT bank across cohorts if the written assessment fails. The "Conditional" outcome (banked written result) only applies when the written assessment passes.

**Actions taken**:
- Credential NOT awarded
- Individual debrief explains both component results and the specific areas requiring development
- Participant must attend the **next scheduled T3 cohort in full** (both days; no exemptions for prior attendance)
- Nexara Master Trainer provides the next cohort's trainer with the participant's domain scores for targeted coaching

**No limit on retake attempts** — however, if a participant fails three T3 cohorts, Nexara L&D will conduct a suitability review before permitting further enrolment.

---

### Outcome 4: FULL RETAKE (Both Fail)

**Criteria**: Written < 75% AND Delivery < 70% (or domain minimums not met)

As Outcome 3 above. No differentiation in process — both fail = full retake.

## 4. Annual Renewal

The credential validity period is **12 months from the issue date**. Certified trainers must complete renewal before the expiry date to maintain their training licence.

### Renewal Options

**Option A — Online Refresher (Preferred)**
- 4-hour online module covering programme updates since the previous renewal year
- Includes a 10-MCQ assessment (≥ 70% to pass — 7 out of 10)
- Available via the Training Portal from 60 days before the renewal date
- Can be completed in one or two sessions (progress is saved)
- On passing: credential automatically renewed for a further 12 months

**Option B — T3 Day 2 Attendance**
- Attend the next scheduled T3 Day 2 as an **observer participant** (not subject to formal delivery assessment)
- Complete the written assessment (standard 20 MCQ; ≥ 75% required)
- On passing: credential renewed for a further 12 months

### Renewal Failure

If the renewal assessment is not passed:
- Option A: one resubmission permitted within 14 days
- Option B: must attend the next available T3 Day 2

### Renewal Non-Completion (Lapsed Credential)

If the renewal deadline passes without completion:

| Time Lapsed | Status | Reinstatement Path |
|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| 0–30 days past expiry | Warning lapsed | Complete renewal immediately; no penalty |
| 31–90 days past expiry | Suspended | Complete renewal; cohort delivery suspended during suspension |
| 91–180 days past expiry | Lapsed | Full T3 retake required (both days) |
| > 180 days past expiry | Cancelled | Full T3 required; existing certificates issued during lapse reviewed |

**Certificates issued during a lapsed period**: Participant certificates issued while the trainer's credential is lapsed are invalid Nexara certifications. Nexara takes no liability for invalid certifications issued during lapsed periods. Trainers are responsible for monitoring their renewal status.

## 5. Credential Withdrawal

Nexara reserves the right to withdraw the "Nexara Certified Internal Trainer" credential in the following circumstances. Withdrawal is communicated in writing by the Nexara Head of L&D.

| Grounds | Process |
|---------|---------|
| Confirmed co-branding or IP violation | Written warning first; second offence → withdrawal |
| Deliberate assessment integrity breach | Immediate withdrawal without warning |
| Delivery of materials outside licensed organisation scope | Written warning first; second offence → withdrawal |
| Three consecutive annual renewal failures | Credential cancelled; reinstatement requires full T3 |
| Confirmed misrepresentation of certification status | Immediate withdrawal without warning |

**Appeals process**: A withdrawn credential may be appealed within 30 calendar days of the withdrawal notification. Appeals are submitted in writing to the Nexara Head of L&D. The appeal is reviewed by a panel of two Nexara L&D staff not involved in the original withdrawal decision. The panel's decision is final.

## 6. Record of Certification

Nexara L&D maintains a central certified trainer register containing:

| Field | Retention |
|-------|----------|
| Participant name | Permanent |
| Organisation | Permanent |
| T3 cohort date | Permanent |
| Written assessment score (%) | Permanent |
| Delivery assessment scores by domain | Permanent |
| Certification date | Permanent |
| Renewal status and dates | Current + 2 previous years |
| Resubmission history (if any) | Permanent |
| Withdrawal history (if any) | Permanent |

Certified trainers may request their record at any time via training@nexara.io. Client organisations may request confirmation of a trainer's certification status (pass/fail + renewal status only; individual scores not disclosed without trainer consent).
