# Train-the-Trainer — Certification Criteria

**Programme**: Nexara IMS Train-the-Trainer (T3)
**Credential awarded**: Nexara Certified Internal Trainer — End User & Module Owner Programmes
**Version**: 1.0

---

## 1. Overview

The "Nexara Certified Internal Trainer — End User & Module Owner Programmes" credential is awarded when a participant **passes both assessment components** during the T3 programme. The two components are independent: a different outcome on each requires a different resubmission pathway. Neither component can substitute for the other.

---

## 2. Assessment Components

### Component A — Written Assessment

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| Format | 20 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) |
| Duration | 30 minutes (strictly timed) |
| Delivery | Training Portal — timed web assessment; or printed paper administered under invigilation |
| Pass threshold | ≥ 75% (15 out of 20 correct) |
| Topics | Adult learning theory (Q1–4), facilitation skills (Q5–8), Nexara curriculum (Q9–12), assessment delivery (Q13–16), programme management (Q17–20) |
| Answer key | `02-day-2-delivery-certification/ANSWER-KEY-WRITTEN.md` (Master Trainer only) |
| When taken | Day 2, 15:15–15:45 |
| Attempt | One attempt per T3 cohort; resubmission at next scheduled cohort only |

### Component B — Delivery Assessment

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| Format | Observed 20-minute training segment |
| Content | Participant selects any segment from End User or Module Owner curriculum; segment assigned by Nexara on Day 1 afternoon |
| Duration | 20 minutes (strictly timed; +2 min grace period) |
| Assessment tool | `03-trainer-certification/DELIVERY-OBSERVER-CHECKLIST.md` |
| Pass threshold | Overall average ≥ 2.5 (70% of 4-point maximum) AND minimum domain scores met |
| Observer | Nexara Master Trainer delivering the T3 programme |
| When taken | Day 2, 13:00–15:00 (observed delivery slots, scheduled per cohort) |
| Resubmission | If pass Written + fail Delivery: resubmit delivery-only within 30 days (see Section 4) |

---

## 3. Certification Outcomes

| Written | Delivery | Outcome |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Pass (≥75%) | Pass (≥70%) | **CERTIFIED** — Credential awarded; certificate issued same session |
| Pass (≥75%) | Fail (<70%) | **CONDITIONAL** — Written result banked; delivery resubmission within 30 days |
| Fail (<75%) | Pass (≥70%) | **PARTIAL** — Delivery result noted but not banked; full retake at next T3 cohort |
| Fail (<75%) | Fail (<70%) | **FULL RETAKE** — Both components must be retaken at next scheduled T3 cohort |

**Important**: The "Partial" pathway (delivery pass, written fail) does not bank the delivery result. Both components must be attempted in the same T3 cohort or resubmission window.

---

## 4. Resubmission Policy

### 4.1 Delivery Resubmission (Conditional pathway)

A participant who passes the written assessment but fails the delivery assessment may resubmit delivery within **30 calendar days** of the original T3 cohort.

**Resubmission process**:
1. The Master Trainer notifies Nexara L&D (training@nexara.io) by end of Day 2 with the participant's name, written result, and delivery scores by domain
2. Nexara schedules a 45-minute resubmission session (virtual or on-site) within 30 days
3. The participant delivers a **different 20-minute segment** from the one used in the original assessment
4. The same observer checklist is used; the same pass threshold applies
5. If the resubmission is passed: credential awarded, backdated to original T3 cohort date
6. If the resubmission is failed: the participant must attend the next full T3 cohort (no further resubmissions on the same written result)

**Conditions for resubmission eligibility**:
- Written pass result is valid only for the 30-day window
- If the participant cannot attend within 30 days (illness, travel), a one-time extension of 14 days may be granted by the Nexara Head of L&D — request must be made before the 30-day deadline expires
- The participant must complete at least 4 hours of self-directed practice delivery before the resubmission (self-declared; not verified)

### 4.2 Full Retake

A participant who fails both components, or who fails the written assessment regardless of delivery outcome, must attend the next scheduled T3 cohort in full.

- Previous T3 attendance does not exempt the participant from any session
- The Nexara Master Trainer who delivers the retake cohort will have access to the participant's domain-level scores from the previous attempt (to focus coaching)
- There is no limit on the number of retake attempts; however, if a participant fails three T3 cohorts, Nexara L&D will conduct a suitability review before permitting further enrolment

## 5. Annual Renewal

The "Nexara Certified Internal Trainer" credential must be renewed annually.

| Renewal Option | Specification |
|---------------|--------------|
| **Option A — Refresher session** | 4-hour online refresher covering updates to the End User and Module Owner programmes since the previous renewal; assessment included (10 MCQ, ≥ 70%); available via Training Portal |
| **Option B — T3 Day 2 repeat** | Attend the next scheduled T3 Day 2 as an observer and participate in the written assessment; no delivery assessment required for renewal |

**Renewal deadlines**:
- First renewal: 12 months from original certification date
- Subsequent renewals: 12 months from most recent renewal date
- Nexara will send renewal reminders at 60 days and 14 days before expiry
- Expired credentials: trainer may not deliver cohorts; must complete renewal before resuming

**Renewal consequences for non-renewal**:
- Lapsed credentials are flagged in the Training Portal; participant certificates issued during a lapsed period are not valid Nexara certifications
- Credentials lapsed > 6 months require a full T3 retake, not just renewal

---

## 6. Record-Keeping

Nexara L&D maintains a central register of all certified Internal Trainers, including:

- Certification date
- Renewal status and next renewal deadline
- Component scores (written % and delivery domain averages)
- Delivery resubmission status (if applicable)
- Programme licence scope (which client organisation)

Certified trainers can request their record at any time via training@nexara.io. Client organisations can request a confirmation of certification for their internal trainers (pass/fail and current renewal status only; individual scores not disclosed without trainer consent).

---

## 7. Withdrawal of Certification

Nexara reserves the right to withdraw a "Nexara Certified Internal Trainer" credential in the following circumstances:

- Confirmed co-branding or intellectual property violation
- Deliberate assessment integrity breach (e.g. sharing questions from the assessment bank)
- Delivery of materials outside the licensed organisation scope without Nexara approval
- Three consecutive annual renewal failures

Withdrawal of certification is communicated in writing by the Nexara Head of L&D. The trainer may appeal via the Nexara formal grievance process within 30 days of notification.
