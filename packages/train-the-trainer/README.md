# Nexara IMS — Train-the-Trainer Programme

**Version 1.0 | Two-Day Instructor-Led Programme | CPD: 14 hours | Dual Assessment**

---

## Overview

The Nexara Train-the-Trainer (T3) Programme equips a client organisation's designated internal trainers to deliver the **End User Training** and **Module Owner Training** programmes independently — using Nexara's editable source materials, facilitation guides, and assessment tools. Upon successful completion of both a written examination and an observed delivery assessment, participants are awarded the **Nexara Certified Internal Trainer — End User & Module Owner Programmes** credential.

This programme is delivered by a Nexara Master Trainer and is limited to **a maximum of 8 participants per cohort** to ensure sufficient assessed delivery time for each candidate.

## Assessment & Certification

| Component | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| Written assessment | 20 MCQ, 30 minutes, ≥ 75% (15/20) to pass |
| Delivery assessment | 20-minute observed segment, 5 competency domains, 4-point scale, ≥ 70% overall, minimum domain scores apply |
| Both required | "Nexara Certified Internal Trainer — End User & Module Owner Programmes" awarded only on passing both |
| Conditional resubmission | Written pass + delivery fail → resubmit delivery within 30 days |
| Full retake | Both fail → next scheduled T3 cohort |
| Annual renewal | 4-hour refresher session OR attendance at next T3 Day 2 |

## Curriculum Handover

On programme completion, certified trainers receive:

- The complete **End User Training** source package (`packages/end-user-training/`)
- The complete **Module Owner Training** source package (`packages/module-owner-training/`) — all 5 one-day programmes
- All assessment banks (participant-facing files only; answer keys remain Nexara-controlled)
- Facilitation guides, lab scenario data sets, and slide templates
- Co-branding assets (within the limits defined in `03-trainer-certification/CO-BRANDING-GUIDE.md`)

Nexara retains intellectual property ownership of all materials. Internal trainers are licensed to deliver within their own organisation only.

## Pre-Delivery Checklist (for Nexara Master Trainers)

1. **Review** `00-programme-overview/COMPETENCY-FRAMEWORK.md` — confirm participant suitability
2. **Read** `01-day-1-foundations/DAY-1-FACILITATION-GUIDE.md` and `02-day-2-delivery-certification/DAY-2-FACILITATION-GUIDE.md` — full scripts
3. **Set up** observed delivery slots in `03-trainer-certification/DELIVERY-OBSERVER-CHECKLIST.md` — one per participant
4. **Prepare** micro-teaching lab (`01-day-1-foundations/LAB-DAY-1.md`) — collect 8 pre-assigned segments
5. **Print** the observer checklist and written assessment paper (or load the portal version)
6. **Confirm** curriculum handover packages are ready for day-end transfer
