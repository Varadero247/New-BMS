# Internal Trainer Handbook

**Programme**: Nexara IMS Train-the-Trainer (T3)
**Version**: 1.0
**For**: Certified internal trainers — ongoing reference document

---

## 1. Introduction

This handbook is your primary reference for day-to-day training delivery as a Nexara Certified Internal Trainer. It covers the pre-session checklist, escalation paths to Nexara, and common situations you will encounter when running End User and Module Owner cohorts.

Keep this document accessible at every session — either on your device or as a printed reference card.

## 2. Pre-Session Checklist

Complete the following at least 24 hours before each session, and again 60 minutes before start.

### 24 Hours Before

| Task | Done? |
|------|-------|
| Confirm room booking and room capacity | ☐ |
| Confirm participant list and enrolment in Training Portal | ☐ |
| Send pre-programme reminder email to all participants (if not sent by portal auto-mailer) | ☐ |
| Log in to the Nexara sandbox and confirm access is working | ☐ |
| Confirm all participants have sandbox credentials (check enrolment record) | ☐ |
| Review the session schedule and module content for the programme being delivered | ☐ |
| Identify the peer feedback forms, formative checks, or assessment papers required | ☐ |
| Check Nexara Training Portal for any system notifications or outage warnings | ☐ |
| Confirm the assessment bank is loaded and the assessment is configured correctly | ☐ |

### 60 Minutes Before

| Task | Done? |
|------|-------|
| Set up the room (furniture, projector/screen, name cards) | ☐ |
| Log in to the sandbox on the trainer device; confirm demo data is present | ☐ |
| Print or confirm digital access to: participant handouts, lab scenarios, feedback forms | ☐ |
| Set up facilitator materials on trainer device (facilitation guide, answer key in secure vault) | ☐ |
| Test AV: projector, remote clicker, sound (if using video) | ☐ |
| Place participant materials (handouts, feedback forms) face-down at seats | ☐ |
| Prepare flipchart: write "Parking Lot" on one sheet, session agenda on another | ☐ |
| Set a timer on your phone for session start | ☐ |

## 3. Session Execution — Quick Reference

### Opening (First 5 Minutes)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Welcome the group; state your name and your role as a certified Nexara trainer |
| 2 | Introduce the session (what they'll be able to do by the end — not just what you'll cover) |
| 3 | Housekeeping: breaks, fire exits, toilets, devices, questions |
| 4 | Activate prior knowledge: one question that connects to their experience |
| 5 | Set the psychological contract: what you commit to them; what you need from them |

### Managing Time During Delivery

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| On schedule | Continue as planned |
| Running 5 minutes early | Use extension questions from the lab file; do not rush the next segment |
| Running 5 minutes behind | Trim the next Q&A segment; do not cut the sandbox exercise |
| Running 10+ minutes behind | Notify the group at the next natural break; adjust the afternoon schedule; do not sacrifice the assessment window |
| Running very late (30+ min behind on a full-day session) | Contact Nexara for advice on whether to continue or reschedule |

### Closing (Last 5 Minutes)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Recap: "Three things we covered today…" (keep it to three) |
| 2 | Reflection: "Write down one thing you'll do differently next week" |
| 3 | Logistics: assessment results, certificates, resubmission (if applicable) |
| 4 | Thank the group; confirm next steps |
| 5 | Collect any outstanding assessment papers or feedback forms |

## 4. Common Situations and How to Handle Them

### Participant arrives 20+ minutes late

- Brief them quietly at the next break; do not interrupt the session for late arrivals
- Do not repeat content you have already covered (disrupts the other participants)
- If they miss the assessment window: they cannot take the assessment today; schedule a separate assessment session

### Participant refuses to use the sandbox ("I'll just watch")

- Acknowledge: "I understand — it can feel unfamiliar at first."
- Redirect: "Try the first step. If it's not working for you after 5 minutes, we'll adjust."
- If persistent: let them observe for the first exercise; involve them directly in the second one by asking them to call out the next step

### A participant challenges the accuracy of Nexara content

- Listen fully; do not dismiss
- If you are confident: explain the correct position with reference to the source material
- If you are uncertain: "That's a fair question — let me verify and confirm by email after the session." Note it on the Parking Lot.
- Do not guess or speculate about platform behaviour — incorrect information is worse than deferred information

### The sandbox is unavailable

- Announce calmly: "We have a technical issue with the sandbox — let's keep going with the module content and I'll make sure we come back to the hands-on exercises."
- Use pre-prepared screenshots of the relevant screens (these should be in your `/slides/` folder as backup slides)
- Contact training@nexara.io with "URGENT: Sandbox Outage — [Org] — [Date]" in the subject

### A participant fails the assessment

- Speak to them privately; never announce a fail publicly
- Follow the communication script in `02-day-2-delivery-certification/MODULE-ASSESSMENT-DELIVERY.md` Section 4
- Explain the resubmission pathway clearly and in writing (email summary after the session)
- Log the outcome in the Training Portal (non-pass status; resubmission pending)

### Two participants are having a side conversation during your delivery

- Pause; make eye contact with them
- If continuing: "Is everything okay? Do you have a question for the group?" — invite them to contribute
- If persistent: speak to them at the break privately; ask them to keep side conversations for the break

### A participant questions whether the programme is worth their time

- Do not argue the value of compliance training — this creates defensiveness
- Acknowledge: "I hear that. Let's make sure the time is as useful as possible for you."
- Connect specifically to their role: ask them what their biggest compliance challenge is, then show directly how the relevant Nexara module addresses it

## 5. Assessment Administration Quick Reference

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| Before assessment | Confirm all participants are logged in (portal) or have papers (printed) |
| Briefing | Use the standard invigilation script (see `MODULE-ASSESSMENT-DELIVERY.md`) |
| Start | Confirm everyone is ready; click "Begin" or say "Begin now" |
| During | Maintain presence in the room; no content questions answered during assessment |
| 25-min mark | "Five minutes remaining" |
| 30-min mark | "Time — please stop now" |
| After | Collect papers or confirm portal submissions; mark (if paper); communicate results 1:1 |
| Certificate | Issue via Training Portal for all pass participants; confirm email delivery |
| Failed outcomes | Private communication; email follow-up with resubmission information |
| Records | Submit completion data to Nexara within 5 business days |

## 7. Annual Renewal Reminder

Your Nexara Certified Internal Trainer credential must be renewed **12 months from your certification date**. Nexara will send reminders at:
- **60 days before expiry**: renewal options communicated; online refresher available from this date
- **14 days before expiry**: final reminder; suspension warning
- **On expiry**: credential lapses; cohort delivery is suspended until renewal is complete

**To renew**: Log in to the Training Portal → Dashboard → Renewal → choose Option A (4-hour online refresher) or Option B (T3 Day 2 attendance). Complete before the deadline.
