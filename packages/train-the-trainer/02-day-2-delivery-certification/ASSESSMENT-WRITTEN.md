# Written Assessment — Train-the-Trainer Programme

**Format**: 20 MCQ | **Time**: 30 minutes | **Pass**: ≥ 75% (15/20) | **Delivery**: Training Portal or printed paper
**Day**: Day 2, 15:15–15:45

*This file contains the 20 questions and answer options. Answers and explanations are in `ANSWER-KEY-WRITTEN.md` (NEXARA MASTER TRAINER ONLY — not distributed to participants).*

---

## Instructions for Participants

You have 30 minutes to complete 20 multiple-choice questions covering adult learning theory, facilitation skills, assessment delivery, programme management, and the Nexara curriculum. Select **one answer** per question. You may review and change answers before submitting. The timer starts when you click **Begin Assessment** (or when the trainer says "Begin" for paper delivery).

A score of **75% or above (15 correct)** is required to pass. Your written result, combined with your delivery assessment, determines your certification outcome.
