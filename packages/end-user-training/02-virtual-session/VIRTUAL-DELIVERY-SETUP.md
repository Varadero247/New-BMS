# Virtual Delivery Setup — End User Training

---

## Pre-Session Checklist (Complete 2 business days before)

### Sandbox Environment

- [ ] Confirm sandbox URL with Nexara Training team
- [ ] Verify sandbox has fictional employee accounts loaded (minimum 20 accounts)
- [ ] Verify all 6 modules are accessible: H&S (incidents + observations + permits), Training, Documents, Dashboard
- [ ] Test incident creation, observation submission, training acknowledgement, and PTW request in sandbox
- [ ] Confirm all sandbox logins are cleared (no prior exercise data from previous cohort)

### Participant Credentials

- [ ] Request sandbox login credentials for all confirmed participants from Nexara Training
- [ ] Send credentials to participants at least 24 hours before the session
- [ ] Include setup instructions (see template below)
- [ ] Confirm receipt from each participant

### Technical Requirements for Participants

Communicate these requirements to participants in advance:

- **Device**: Laptop or desktop computer (phone/tablet not recommended for exercises)
- **Browser**: Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (latest version)
- **Internet**: Minimum 5 Mbps download speed
- **Audio/Video**: Working microphone and camera (headset recommended)
- **Setup**: Have the invitation meeting link and sandbox URL open in separate browser tabs before the session starts

## Facilitator Technology Setup

### Nexara Facilitator Account

The facilitator should use a dedicated facilitator account in the sandbox (not a participant account). The facilitator account should have:
- All 6 modules accessible
- At least 3 assigned training items (to demonstrate the training module)
- A pre-recorded incident (to demonstrate the record status page)
- A PTW in "Pending Approval" status (to demonstrate the live permits view)

Contact Nexara Training Support (training-support@nexara.io) to configure the facilitator sandbox account.

### Screen and Audio

- Use an external monitor if possible (for side-by-side view of sandbox and facilitation guide)
- Use a headset for audio — laptop speakers and microphones produce poor quality for virtual delivery
- Close all non-essential applications before the session (reduces meeting and sandbox lag)
- Disable system notifications during the session (calendar alerts, emails)
