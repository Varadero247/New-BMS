# Co-Branding Guide — End User Training Certificates

---

## What Is Permitted

Client organisations may co-brand:
1. The End User completion certificate (client logo in bottom-right co-branding zone)
2. Virtual session slides (client logo in slide footer, right-aligned)
3. E-learning module title screens (client logo in bottom-right)
4. Session invitation emails (client branding in footer area)

---

## Logo Placement Rules

**On certificates**:
- Position: bottom-right co-branding zone only
- Maximum size: 40mm wide × 20mm tall
- Minimum separation from Nexara seal: 5mm
- Format: High-resolution PNG or SVG

**On slides and e-learning**:
- Position: bottom-right, proportional to and never larger than the Nexara logo
- Never on the same line as the Nexara wordmark

---

## Organisation Name Field

The `Organisation` field on each certificate is populated with the client organisation's name. This is the only text addition permitted. The field displays as:

> "Organisation: [Client Organisation Name]"

This field serves as sufficient attribution for co-delivery — no additional "Delivered by" text is needed or permitted on the certificate itself.

---

## What Is NOT Permitted

1. Removing the Nexara logo or seal from any material
2. Changing the programme title (must always read "Nexara Platform Foundation — End User Training Programme")
3. Altering the pass threshold (80%)
4. Changing the CPD hour declaration (4 hours)
5. Adding third-party organisation logos (consultancies, technology partners)
6. Using client colours on Nexara-branded elements

---

## Approval Process

Submit client logo files to training@nexara.io at least 5 business days before delivery. Nexara returns the approved co-branded templates within 2 business days. Do not modify templates after approval.
