# Summative Assessment — End User Training

**Format**: 20 MCQ | **Time**: 20 minutes (virtual); untimed (e-learning) | **Pass**: ≥ 80% (16/20)
**Delivery**: Web portal `/end-user/assessment`

---

## Instructions

You have 20 questions covering all six modules. In the virtual session, you have 20 minutes. In the e-learning version, there is no time limit. Select one answer per question. You may review and change your answers before submitting. A score of 80% or above (16 correct) earns your Nexara Platform Foundation — End User Completion Certificate.

---

## Questions

**Q1.** Where in Nexara do you find your assigned training items?
- A) Health & Safety → Training Records
- B) Training module in the left sidebar navigation
- C) Profile Settings → My Learning
- D) Dashboard → System Notifications

**Q2.** A worker trips and falls, spraining their wrist and requiring medical treatment at a clinic. No hospitalisation. Which record type and severity applies?
- A) Near Miss — MINOR
- B) Incident — MINOR
- C) Incident — MODERATE
- D) Observation — HIGH

**Q3.** The `dateOccurred` field on an incident record should contain:
- A) The date the investigation was opened
- B) The date the incident was first reported to your manager
- C) The date and time the incident actually happened
- D) Today's date — when you are creating the record

**Q4.** After submitting an incident report, the status shows "Under Investigation". This means:
- A) The system is checking for errors in your record
- B) Your HSE manager has opened an investigation into the incident
- C) Your report was rejected — you need to resubmit
- D) The incident has been closed with no action required

**Q5.** A mandatory training item shows amber on your training dashboard. What is the most appropriate action?
- A) Wait until it turns red before taking action
- B) Contact your manager to request an exemption
- C) Complete the training before the deadline within the next 30 days
- D) Ignore it — amber status items are optional

**Q6.** You've completed an external first aid course. How do you update your training record in Nexara?
- A) Your training provider automatically sends a completion record to Nexara
- B) HR updates your record based on your expense claim
- C) Training → find the course → Record External Completion → enter date, result, upload certificate
- D) Send your certificate to your line manager — they update the system

**Q7.** Which of the following work activities requires a Permit to Work in Nexara's standard configuration?
- A) Routine office maintenance (replacing a broken desk drawer)
- B) Entry into a confined space (vessel or tank)
- C) Cleaning windows on the ground floor
- D) Using a hand drill to hang a picture

**Q8.** Before starting work under an approved PTW, what must you do?
- A) Start work immediately — approval means all checks are done
- B) Call the HSE manager to verbally confirm approval
- C) Open the permit, read the permit conditions carefully, and confirm pre-work checks in the system
- D) Print the permit and display it at the worksite

**Q9.** Your permit is approved from 09:00 to 14:00. At 13:30, the task is taking longer than expected. What should you do?
- A) Continue working — finishing 1 hour late is acceptable
- B) Stop, secure the area, and submit a new PTW for additional time before 14:00
- C) Ask a colleague to take over the remaining work
- D) Extend the permit end time yourself in the system

**Q10.** After completing permitted work, closing the permit in Nexara requires confirmation that:
- A) A supervisor has physically inspected the work
- B) The work is complete, the area is safe, and no hazards remain
- C) The permit has been printed and filed in the site office
- D) A CAPA has been raised for any issues found during the work

**Q11.** A PTW is rejected with the reason: "Work description too vague — insufficient detail." The correct response is:
- A) Submit the same form to a different approver
- B) Escalate to the HSE Manager to override the rejection
- C) Rewrite the work description with specific details and resubmit
- D) Proceed without the permit — the work is urgent

**Q12.** You notice a puddle forming under a machine in the production area. No one has slipped. Which record type is most appropriate?
- A) Incident — MINOR
- B) Near Miss
- C) Observation (negative)
- D) No record needed — it will be cleaned up soon

**Q13.** Which of the following is a positive observation?
- A) A blocked fire exit
- B) Missing PPE in the storage area
- C) A colleague stopping a job because they weren't confident it was safe to proceed
- D) A machine guard that is not properly secured

**Q14.** Submitting an observation anonymously in Nexara means:
- A) The observation is not received by anyone — it goes to a void
- B) Your name is not visible on the observation record, but it is still received and actioned by the HSE team
- C) The observation is automatically closed with no investigation
- D) You can only submit anonymous observations if your manager has approved it

**Q15.** Your personal compliance dashboard shows a red status. The most likely cause is:
- A) Your password is about to expire
- B) You have not logged in for 7 days
- C) At least one mandatory training item is overdue or expired

**Q16.** A regulatory inspector visits your site and asks to confirm your manual handling training is complete. The quickest and most reliable response is:
- A) Tell them you completed it last year but can't remember the exact date
- B) Ask HR to pull your training records
- C) Log in to Nexara and show them your training history report directly

**Q17.** Which report in Nexara shows all the incidents and near misses you have submitted in a specific period?

**Q18.** You receive a notification: "Your Fire Safety certificate expires in 25 days." What is the correct response?

- B) Ask your manager to extend the certificate validity in the system

**Q19.** A fellow employee has completed all their mandatory training but their dashboard still shows amber. The most likely explanation is:
- A) There is a system bug that requires IT support
