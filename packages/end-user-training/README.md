# Nexara IMS — End User Training Programme

**Version 1.0 | 4 CPD Hours | Two Delivery Formats: Virtual Instructor-Led & Self-Paced E-Learning**

---

## Overview

The End User Training Programme gives operational staff the task-level skills to use Nexara IMS confidently and correctly in their day-to-day roles. No prior system knowledge or technical background is required. On completion, participants earn the **Nexara Platform Foundation — End User Completion Certificate**.

## Target Audience

- Operational staff using Nexara IMS to record incidents, near misses, and observations
- Workers who must acknowledge assigned training and procedures
- Personnel operating under Permit-to-Work controls
- Any employee who needs to navigate the Nexara platform and access their personal compliance dashboard

## Delivery Formats

### Virtual Instructor-Led (4 hours)
A live, facilitated session via Zoom or Microsoft Teams. Participants work through guided exercises in the Nexara sandbox environment. See `02-virtual-session/` for full delivery materials.

### Self-Paced E-Learning
Six SCORM-compatible e-learning modules followed by a summative assessment. Participants complete each module at their own pace. See `03-elearning/` for design specifications and storyboard outlines.

## Pre-Delivery Checklist

1. **Read** `00-programme-overview/FACILITATOR-GUIDE.md` for pacing and inclusivity guidance
2. **Confirm** sandbox environment access for all participants (see `02-virtual-session/VIRTUAL-DELIVERY-SETUP.md`)
3. **Share** login credentials for the training environment in advance
4. **Print** or distribute the competency checklist (`04-assessments/COMPETENCY-CHECKLIST.md`) for line manager sign-off
