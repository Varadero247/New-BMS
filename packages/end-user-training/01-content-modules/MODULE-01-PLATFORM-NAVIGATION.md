# Module 1: Platform Navigation

**Duration**: 30 minutes | **Format**: Live demo (virtual or on-site)

---

## Overview

This module orients participants to the Nexara IMS platform. By the end, every participant can log in, find their modules, understand notifications, and update their profile settings.

---

## Part 1: Logging In (5 min)

### Your Login URL

Your organisation's Nexara IMS is accessed at: `https://[your-organisation].nexara.io`

Your credentials were sent to your work email when your account was created. If you haven't received them, contact your IT helpdesk or HR.

### First Login

1. Go to your organisation's Nexara URL
2. Enter your email address and the temporary password from your welcome email
3. You'll be prompted to set a new password — choose something memorable with at least 8 characters, a number, and a symbol
4. You'll be taken to the **Dashboard** — your home screen in Nexara

### Forgotten Password

If you forget your password: click **Forgot Password** on the login screen → enter your email → you'll receive a reset link within 5 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact IT.

---

## Part 2: Dashboard Orientation (8 min)

When you log in, you see the **Dashboard**. Let's explore what's on screen:

### Navigation Bar (top)

- **Nexara logo** (top-left): Clicking this takes you back to the Dashboard from anywhere
- **Module name** (top bar): The name of the module you're currently in
- **Notification bell**: Shows the number of unread notifications — alerts about your training, incidents you reported, and permits you're involved in
- **Search icon**: Global search — type anything to find records across all modules you have access to
- **Profile avatar** (top-right): Your name and photo. Click to access profile settings, change password, or log out

### Sidebar Navigation (left)

- **Home/Dashboard**: Your personal overview
- **Module icons**: Each module you have access to appears here — H&S (hard hat icon), Training (graduation cap), Documents (folder), etc.
- **Collapse arrow**: You can hide the sidebar labels to give more screen space

### Personal Dashboard Widgets

Your dashboard shows personalised information:
- **My Training**: Courses assigned to you and their completion status
- **My Actions**: Tasks awaiting your action (e.g. training to complete, permits to respond to)
- **Recent Activity**: Your last 5 actions in the system
- **Compliance Status**: Your personal RAG (Red/Amber/Green) status

---

## Part 3: Module Switching (5 min)

Nexara is organised into modules — each covering a different function. You will only see the modules relevant to your role.

**To switch modules**: Click the relevant icon in the sidebar, or click the module's name in the dropdown if you're on the dashboard.

**Common modules for end users**:
| Module | What you use it for |
|--------|-------------------|
| Health & Safety | Recording incidents, near misses, observations, permits |
| Training | Finding and completing assigned training |
| Documents | Reading controlled procedures |

You do not need to know all modules — only the ones in your sidebar.

---

## Part 4: Notifications (7 min)

The notification bell (🔔) is important — it's how Nexara communicates directly with you.

### Types of Notifications You'll Receive

| Notification | What it means | What to do |
|-------------|---------------|-----------|
| Training assigned | A new course has been added to your training list | Navigate to Training → complete it before the deadline |
| Training due soon | A mandatory course is due in 7 days | Complete the training |
| Training overdue | A mandatory course deadline has passed | Complete immediately and notify your manager |
| Permit approved | A PTW you submitted has been approved | Review conditions before starting work |
| Permit rejected | A PTW you submitted has been returned | Read the rejection reason and resubmit or escalate |
| Action assigned | Someone has assigned you an action (e.g. corrective action from an inspection finding) | Review and complete by the due date |

**Clicking a notification** takes you directly to the relevant record.

---

## Part 5: Profile Settings (5 min)

Click your profile avatar (top-right) → **Profile Settings**.

Things you should set up:
1. **Profile photo**: Optional but helps colleagues identify you in the system
2. **Contact details**: Your mobile number — used for urgent notifications
3. **Notification preferences**: You can choose to receive notifications by email, in-app, or both
4. **Language**: If Nexara is available in your preferred language, set it here

**What you cannot change**: Your email address, department, job title, or permission level. These are managed by HR and your Nexara administrator.
