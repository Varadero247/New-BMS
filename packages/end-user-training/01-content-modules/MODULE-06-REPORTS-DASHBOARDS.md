# Module 6: Reports & Dashboards

**Duration**: 25 minutes | **Format**: Live demo

---

## Overview

Nexara provides a personal compliance dashboard that shows your current status at a glance. This module covers what the dashboard shows you, how to interpret RAG status, how to run a pre-configured report, and when you might be asked to export your compliance data.

---

## Part 1: Your Personal Compliance Dashboard (10 min)

### Accessing the Dashboard

Click **Home** (Nexara logo) to return to the Dashboard at any time.

Your dashboard has several widgets — the most relevant for end users:

### Training Compliance Widget

Shows your overall training compliance status:
- Number of courses assigned
- Number completed ✓
- Number overdue ✗
- Number due soon ⚠

Click **View All** to see the full list with individual statuses.

### My Actions Widget

Things that are waiting for you to do something:
- Training not yet started
- Permits awaiting your closure
- Observations you submitted that have a follow-up action for you
- Any other tasks assigned to you in the system

### Recent Submissions Widget

Your last 5 submissions (incidents, observations, permits, training completions). Click any item to open the full record.

## Part 2: Understanding RAG Status (5 min)

RAG = Red, Amber, Green. You'll see this throughout Nexara:

| Colour | Training meaning | What to do |
|--------|-----------------|-----------|
| 🟢 Green | All mandatory training complete, no items expiring soon | Nothing required |
| 🟡 Amber | 1 or more items expiring within 30 days | Complete the amber items this week |
| 🔴 Red | 1 or more items overdue or expired | Treat as urgent — complete and notify your manager |

**Your manager sees your RAG**: Your compliance dashboard status is visible to your line manager. If you're red, they will be notified automatically.

**How to go from Red to Green**: Complete the overdue item(s) in the system. Once recorded as complete, your status updates automatically within minutes.

## Part 3: Pre-Configured Reports (7 min)

Some reports have been pre-configured for your role. To access them: click **Reports** in the sidebar (if visible) or **Reports** from the relevant module.

**Reports you may have access to** (varies by role and configuration):

| Report | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| My Training History | All training completions, dates, scores |
| My Incident Records | All incidents/near misses you have submitted |
| My Observations | All observations you have submitted |
| My Permits | All PTW requests you have submitted |

To generate a report:
1. Click the report name
2. Select a date range if prompted
3. Click **Generate**
4. View on-screen or click **Export** to download as PDF or CSV

## Part 4: When You Might Be Asked to Export Your Compliance Data (3 min)

There are common situations where you may be asked to provide a compliance report:

**Regulatory inspection**: If your site is inspected, your manager may ask you to confirm your training is up to date. Log in and show your training compliance dashboard.

**Annual appraisal**: Some organisations use training compliance as part of performance review. Export your training history report as a PDF.

**CQC/ISO/OHSAS audit**: If your organisation is being audited, auditors may ask to see that specific staff have completed specific training. Your HR or quality team will pull this information — you don't need to do anything.

**New role**: When moving to a new role, HR may ask you to confirm you've completed all role-specific training. Export your training history.

**Important**: Your data belongs to your employer. Your organisation's privacy policy explains how it is used and retained. If you have questions, speak with HR.
