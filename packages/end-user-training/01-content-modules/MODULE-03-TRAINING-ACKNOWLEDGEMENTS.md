# Module 3: Training Acknowledgements

**Duration**: 30 minutes | **Format**: Guided exercise

---

## Overview

Nexara tracks your mandatory training. This module shows you how to find your assigned training, read or complete it, and record your acknowledgement. It also explains what the green/amber/red status indicators mean.

---

## Part 1: Finding Your Assigned Training (8 min)

### Where to Find It

Click **Training** in the left sidebar. You'll see your **Training Dashboard** with:

- **My Training List**: All courses and procedures assigned to you
- **Overdue**: Any items past their completion deadline (shown in red)
- **Due Soon**: Items due within the next 30 days (shown in amber)
- **Completed**: Items you've finished

You can also see a summary on your personal dashboard home page.

### What Gets Assigned to You

| Type | Examples |
|------|---------|
| Mandatory training | Fire safety, manual handling, data protection, induction |
| Procedure acknowledgements | Updated site rules, revised work procedures, policy changes |
| Refresher training | Annual renewals of certificates about to expire |
| Role-specific training | Courses required for your specific job function |

Your line manager or HR team assigns training to you. If you see training you believe is assigned in error, contact HR.

---

## Part 2: Completing a Training Item (12 min)

### For a Procedure Acknowledgement

1. Click on the procedure name in your training list
2. The procedure document opens (PDF viewer or text)
3. Read the full procedure
4. Scroll to the bottom — click **I have read and understood this procedure**
5. Enter today's date in the confirmation field
6. Click **Confirm Acknowledgement**

The item moves from your training list to **Completed** with a timestamp.

### For a Training Course with a Knowledge Check

1. Click on the course name
2. The course content loads (slides, video, or text)
3. Work through all the content
4. At the end, complete the knowledge check — typically 5 questions
5. Submit your answers
6. If you score above the pass mark: click **Complete Course** — a completion record is saved
7. If you don't pass the knowledge check: the feedback shows which answers were incorrect — review and retry

### For an External Training Certificate

If you've completed training externally (e.g. a first aid course):
1. Find the course in your training list — it may be pre-assigned awaiting your completion record
2. Click **Record External Completion**
3. Enter the date completed and your result
4. Upload your certificate (PDF or photo)
5. Click **Save**

## Part 3: Understanding Your Compliance Status (5 min)

Training compliance is shown with a colour-coded RAG (Red/Amber/Green) status:

| Colour | Meaning | What you should do |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| 🟢 Green | All mandatory training complete and valid | Nothing required |
| 🟡 Amber | A course is due within 30 days, or a certificate is expiring soon | Complete it before the deadline |
| 🔴 Red | A course is overdue or a certificate has expired | Complete immediately and notify your manager |

**Your manager can see your status**: Your line manager and HR can see your training compliance. If you're showing red, they will be notified automatically.

---

## Part 4: Compliance Deadline Alerts (5 min)

You'll receive automatic notifications:
- **30 days before a deadline**: Email notification with a link to the training item
- **7 days before a deadline**: Repeat email notification; your line manager is also notified
- **On the deadline day**: Final notification; system records you as overdue if not completed
- **When expired**: Notification that your certificate/completion has lapsed and a refresher is required

If you receive a notification about training you've already completed externally and not yet recorded — record it immediately to keep your status green.
