# End User Training — Learning Objectives

Objectives are written at the **Apply (A)** and **Understand (U)** levels of Bloom's Taxonomy — appropriate for operational task-level training.

---

## Module 1 — Platform Navigation

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. **(A)** Log in to Nexara IMS, reset their password if needed, and navigate to their assigned module from the dashboard
2. **(U)** Explain the purpose of the sidebar navigation, the notification bell, and the profile/settings menu
3. **(A)** Switch between modules using the module switcher and identify the module that handles each task they are responsible for

---

## Module 2 — Recording Incidents

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. **(A)** Record an incident or near miss in Nexara IMS, completing all mandatory fields correctly including `title`, `dateOccurred`, `severity`, `location`, and `immediateActions`
2. **(U)** Distinguish between an incident, a near miss, and an observation — and select the correct record type for a given situation
3. **(A)** Upload photographic evidence and add a witness statement to an incident record

---

## Module 3 — Training Acknowledgements

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. **(A)** Navigate to their assigned training list in Nexara and open an assigned training course or procedure
2. **(A)** Read a procedure or complete a knowledge check and submit an acknowledgement record with the correct date and status
3. **(U)** Interpret their training compliance dashboard — understanding what green, amber, and red completion statuses mean and what action is required for each

---

## Module 4 — Permit to Work

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. **(U)** Identify which types of work activities require a Permit to Work and explain why permits are required before high-risk work begins
2. **(A)** Complete a Permit to Work request form in Nexara with correct details, including hazard identification and proposed control measures
3. **(A)** Locate an approved PTW on the permits dashboard, confirm permit conditions before starting work, and submit a permit closure record when work is complete

---

## Module 5 — Observations

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. **(U)** Distinguish between a positive observation (safe act or condition) and a negative observation (unsafe act or condition) and explain the purpose of recording both types
2. **(A)** Submit an observation record in Nexara including location, category, description, and at least one photograph
3. **(A)** Submit an observation anonymously (where permitted) and track the status of previously submitted observations

---

## Module 6 — Reports & Dashboards

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. **(A)** Access their personal compliance dashboard and identify their outstanding training requirements, open incident records, and permit status
2. **(U)** Interpret RAG (Red/Amber/Green) status indicators on their dashboard and describe what action each colour requires from them
3. **(A)** Export a pre-configured personal compliance report and understand when and why this might be requested by their manager or HR
