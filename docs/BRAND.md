# Nexara Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Brand Name** | Nexara IMS |
| **Previous Name** | Resolvex (deprecated February 2026) |
| **Canonical Domain** | nexara.io |
| **App Domain** | app.nexara.io |
| **API Domain** | api.nexara.io |

## Package Naming

- **Public SDK**: `@nexara/sdk` (published to npm)
- **Internal packages**: `@ims/*` scope (retained for backwards compatibility)

## Tagline

> Every standard. One intelligent platform.

## Brand Colours

| Colour | Hex | Usage |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Navy | `#1E3A8A` | Primary brand, headers |
| Sage Green | `#065F46` | Secondary, accents |
| Teal | `#059669` | Interactive elements, CTAs |
| Dark Background | `#111827` | Dark mode background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Light mode background |
| Gray 400 | `#9CA3AF` | Secondary text |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display (headings) | Syne | 800 |
| Body | DM Sans | 400/500/600 |
| Monospace (code) | JetBrains Mono | 400 |

## Logo

- **Mark**: "N" lettermark or Nexus Node symbol
- **Wordmark**: "Nexara" in Syne 800
- **On dark backgrounds**: White
- **On light backgrounds**: Navy (#1E3A8A)

## Voice and Tone

- Professional, confident, clear
- Mid-senior developer audience
- Avoid jargon; explain ISO standards in plain terms
- Use "platform" not "tool" or "software"

## Legal

- Copyright: `(c) 2026 Nexara Ltd. All rights reserved.`
- Always use "Nexara IMS" (with IMS suffix) in product references
- The name "Resolvex" is deprecated and must not appear in new materials
